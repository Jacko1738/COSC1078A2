Mitchell: The best aspect of the project was the delegation of roles as the tasks at hand were designed in a way that they could be split up, allowing work to be performed efficiently and with little risk of clashing that could be derived from multiple people working on a single piece of information. One aspect that could be improved is communication. Our communication channels took place on multiple platforms being Discord and Microsoft Teams which required both to be monitored to be up to date, it was especially easy to fall behind if meetings were missed due to a lack of written information. This also made retrieval of written information difficult due to the existence of two different channels which would require going through both, an obvious solution is to restrict communication to a single channel. I was surprised how amicable and easy my teammates were to work with, even with my frequent absences. I was apprehensive about working in a team due to my preference for individualism, but they helped make this project a rather pleasant experience. An apparent benefit of working in this group was the variety of ideas that were placed forth before our project was finalised which helped expand my scope and perspective on what is possible within such a project.
